What is Programming?

Programming is the process of giving a computer specific instructions to perform tasks. These instructions, written in special languages like Python or Java, tell the computer what to do, step by step. Programming allows you to create everything from websites and apps to games and software. It's like teaching the computer how to solve problems or automate tasks for you.
